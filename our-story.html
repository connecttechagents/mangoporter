The Brothers Behind the Farm

NetraaNikhilam Farms is run by two brothers with complementary roles that bring a new standard of quality to Bangalore's mango season.

One works full-time on the farm and holds a Master’s degree in Organic Chemistry, applying laboratory-level precision to orchard management. The other manages operations, logistics, and systems from Bangalore, ensuring the tech-driven delivery of our harvest.

Together, they apply scientific discipline to traditional mango farming.

Our Method

Scientific Farming, Not Guesswork

We do not follow “natural” or unverified farming practices. We follow science.

🔬

Food Safety

Our mangoes are grown using approved pesticides and nutrients, used strictly within export-compliant safety limits followed by the USA and Europe.

📊

Consistency

Scientific measurement ensures that every batch meets the same high standard of maturity, density, and sweetness.

🌳

Orchard Health

By using data-driven inputs, we ensure the long-term health of our trees and the sustainability of the land.

Full-time presence

Hands-On Supervision

This is not management over phone calls.

Every nutrient application, pest-control decision, and harvest moment is supervised in person. One of the owners is present at the farm full-time, ensuring that no compromise is made on the standards we've set for the family table.

Why We Deliver Society-Wise

Mangoes are sensitive fruits that do not belong in bulk warehouses.

Community batch drops allow us to minimize handling, avoid storage, and deliver fresher fruit from Chittoor directly to your gate in Bangalore. We believe trust is built through transparency and repeat consistency — not through bulk volume.

  • ✅ No Storage — Farm to gate in hours.
  • ✅ Minimal Handling — Less bruising and damage.
  • ✅ Predictable Quality — Freshness you can see.
