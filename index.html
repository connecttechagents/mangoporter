Direct to Gate

How It Works

From our trees to your community gate in 3 simple steps.

01

Harvested Right

Mangoes are harvested at the correct physiological maturity and delivered firm to prevent transit damage. This ensures they ripen naturally into their full flavor profile.

02

Direct Transit

Fruit moves directly from the farm in Chittoor to Bangalore society gates — no warehouse storage, no multiple loading/unloading, no quality loss.

03

Community Batch Drops

Deliveries are planned society-wise to ensure freshness, speed, and predictable quality. We coordinate through dedicated WhatsApp groups for each community.
